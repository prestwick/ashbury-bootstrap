Mission
Ashbury MDM is the trusted leader in family technology and a thought leader in youth protection on the Internet and managing childrens' identities.

Vision

Mobile devices and social networks have transformed ordinary Internet users from content consumers to content creators. Corporations have managed this well but parents are behind in guiding their childrens' ramp onto the Internet in the mobile era. Young children need parental assistance and guidance to conceal their identities and shield themselves from adult Internet users. Adolescent children need to limit personally identifiable information and manage long term reputational risk. There is no way to delete content and media from the cloud. Powerful technologies are being used to store large volumes of data on us and our children. Parent Cloud was formed to put powerful technology back in the parents' hands.

Values

  1. Customer trust and information security is our entire business
  2. Feature simplicity trumps feature depth
    • Notifications instead of logins
    • Convention instead of administration
  3. Automate every business process and leverage the cloud
    • Engineers: automate builds, tests, monitoring, configuration, deployment
    • Operations: automate data center buildout, network buildout, alerting/paging
    • Corporate: sales force automation, cloud payroll, cloud HR retention/reviews/recruiting
  4. Radical transparency in corporate and engineering operations.
    • Open document policy: share with the whole company and outside world unless legally bound
    • Open source all code, either immediately or with a 12 month delay if sensitive
  5. Volunteerism and community
    • Volunteering our time is more impactful than donating money
    • Make our community better than when we arrived and leverage the Ashbury Foundation
