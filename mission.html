Mission
Empowering parents with tools for privacy, protection, and trust.

Vision

Mobile devices have exponentially increased our access to people and information. Young children need parental assistance and guidance to conceal their identities and shield themselves from adult Internet users. Adolescent children need to limit personally identifiable information and manage long term reputational risk. Its extremely difficult to delete content and media from the internet. Powerful technologies are being used to store large volumes of data on us and our children. By leveraging mature enterprise security software, Parent Cloud puts powerful technology back in the parents' hands.

Values

Simplicity in security

Corporate transparency and personal privacy

Volunteerism and community
